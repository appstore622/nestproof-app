About NestProof

NestProof helps you scan and organize documents, track home inventory and warranties, and export insurance-ready reports. All data stays on your iPhone by default.

  • No account, ads, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking.
  • Optional encrypted backup to your iCloud Drive.
  • Face ID, passcode, or gesture lock.
  • One-time App Store purchase — no subscription.
